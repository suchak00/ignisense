About IGNISENSE

IGNISENSE is an advanced IoT-based natural disaster management and early warning system designed to continuously monitor environmental conditions and provide real-time alerts during emergency situations. The system uses temperature, humidity, soil moisture, and flame sensors to detect disaster risks before they become catastrophic.

The collected data is transmitted to the ThingSpeak cloud platform for real-time visualization and global access through a web-based dashboard. Additionally, a GSM module ensures emergency call alerts even when internet connectivity is unavailable.

IGNISENSE helps reduce disaster response time and enhances safety by integrating IoT sensing, cloud monitoring, and GSM-based alert mechanisms into a single unified system.

Mechanism of Working

The IGNISENSE system continuously collects environmental data using multiple sensors deployed in disaster-prone areas. The DHT22 sensor measures ambient temperature and humidity, while the soil moisture sensor monitors ground water content. A flame sensor instantly detects fire presence.

The ESP32 microcontroller processes sensor readings and compares them with predefined safety thresholds. When abnormal conditions are detected, the system categorizes the situation as a potential disaster risk.

In emergency conditions, the GSM module automatically initiates a call alert to predefined contacts. Simultaneously, sensor data is uploaded to the ThingSpeak cloud, allowing real-time monitoring from anywhere in the world.
